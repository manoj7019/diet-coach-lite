Points to remember :

TDEE - Total Daily Energy Expenditure

BMR - Basal metabolic rate (For most of us - BMR = 50-70% TDEE)

🥱🔥 NEAT - Non-Exercise Activity Thermogenesis (10-30% of TDEE)

🏋🏻‍♂️🔥 EAT - Exercise Activity Thermogenesis (0-30% of TDEE)

🍛🔥 TEF - Thermic effect of food (5-10% of TDEE)


🍗🥦🔥 Protein and Fiber thermogenesis ~ 30%

🍞🧈🔥 Carbs (~5-10%) and Fats thermogenesis (~0-3%)


❗ If haven't lost atleast 50% of goal weight loss target, reduce Carbs & Fats intake by 10%, still no expected result? Reduce another 10%.


Weight Gain

Overfeeding😋 + No resistance training🥱 = 30-50% 🔺 Lean Mass

Overfeeding😋 + Resistance training🏋🏻‍♂️ = 100% 🔺 Lean Mass

✅ New lifter will add Lean Mass at much faster rate than people with 5+ years of consistent resistance training.

✅ We should allow 30% increase in our relative body fat percentage during gaining phase i.e. If we started at 10% Body Fat Percentage, we should go upto 13%.

❗ If we failed to gain weight, increase Carbs and Fats intake by 5-10%.


Body Fat Percentage

❗DXA scans, which use low energy X-rays to determine body fat (this is preferred)

❗Skin calipers, which measure the folds of the skin (most accessible)
